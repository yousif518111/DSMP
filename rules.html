Our rules
Failure to comply with these rules will get you warned, muted and/or banned.

Griefing and killing people without an understandable reason is punishable, you may not grief or kill players on the No kill list.

Using autoclickers for purposes such as pvp makes the game unfair for others and that is why it isn't allowed.

If you are given a punishment by staff, you may only argue it in a ticket on discord and not argue it with staff.

While some moderators may speak a little bit of spanish or arabic or russian, it's not easy to monitor multiple languages at the same time, so please try to speak english only

Stay respectful to everyone and don't use offensive or profane language. This includes using racist slurs and swearing.

We highly advise you don't post any links in the in-game chat or discord chat, but if you had to, please use direct links, this includes google search results, shortened links, image links, etc...

Posting any links to your own discord servers, or a website you own, and any other post or message that may be qualified as self-advertisng is punishable

Your gamertag must be appropriate, this means your gamertag must follow the rules Xbox have set for setting your gamertag.

Don't send messages too fast or repeatedly send the same message multiple times within a short time spam


You may not use harmful bugs that may in any way lag the server or give you unobtainable items.
